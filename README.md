# 🌿 DonaMart

**Premium Eco-Friendly Dona & Pattal Manufacturer**

DonaMart is a modern business website designed for showcasing and selling eco-friendly disposable products such as Dona, Pattal, Areca Leaf Plates, Bowls, and other biodegradable tableware. The platform is built to help customers easily explore products and submit bulk order inquiries.

---

## 📖 About

DonaMart is committed to promoting sustainable and environmentally friendly dining solutions. Our products are made from natural materials and are ideal for homes, restaurants, caterers, event organizers, and wholesale buyers.

The website provides a professional online presence for the factory and simplifies customer interaction through product catalogs and inquiry forms.
